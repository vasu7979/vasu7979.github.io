About Us

Smart retail experience with Nukkad shops

Nukkad Shops offers a one-stop solution for managing every aspect of your bussiness billing, inventory Management, customer engagement, mobile storefront and many more. We offer Android based reliable POS solutions to match every budget and feature request.

key Features

Customer Engagement

    Loyalty program with access to deep analytics
    Sell more with up-sell and cross sell recommendations.
    Create segments and run targeted marketing campaigns

Omni Channel

    Sell online on Nukkad Shops mobile app
    Integrate with third party e-commerce apps

Multiple Store Management

    Manage your stores and purchases centrally
    Access reports centrally
    Enable inter store stock transfer

Epaylater Credit

    Access Free working capital upto 25 lacs in less than 2 minutes and give your kirana business a boost.
    Credit limit upto 25L @ 0% for purchases from Metro, Walmart, LOTS, etc.
    Hassle free process and instant approval within 48 hours.

GST Ready Software

    HSN code and GST rates for more than 50,000 items mapped
    complete tax report break-up of GSTR-1, GSTR-2, GSTR-3 and GSTR-4(composite) with export option for GST filing
    additional CESS calculation on selected products with reports

Procurement

    Make your purchases and get them delivered at your store for free

Day to day operations

    Billing
    With preloaded 50,000+ SKUs with HSN codes
    Transactional SMS for every bill
    Generate your own barcodes and create private labels
    Inventory Management
    Create offers and move dead stock
    Refund and credit management
    Auto GRN integrated with Procurement app to reduce manual effort
    Vendor management
    Accounting and Reporting
    Access to store information on mobile
